A Bit About me

Hi! My name is Frederic Pun. I am a first year Computer Science student at the University of Toronto (Scarborough Campus). Although I was born in Canada, I spent most of my life living in Macau, China. I knew my passion was in computer software so I returned to study my interest at the greatest university in Canada.

Being an international student, I was fortunate enough to have the experience to travel around a fair bit.
